Understand what links are really doing.

Proxuma is a calm, offline-first security understanding tool designed to help people reason about links that feel suspicious — without tracking, fear, or cloud-based scanning.

Why Proxuma exists

Modern scams no longer rely on obvious mistakes. They rely on subtle breaks in normal behavior — extra logins, unexpected redirects, or flows that feel slightly wrong.

Proxuma focuses on recognizing those patterns calmly, so people can make better decisions before they click.
