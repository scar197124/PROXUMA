Privacy

Proxuma was built on a privacy-first model. Your scans run locally in your browser whenever possible. We do not sell scan history. We do not require accounts to scan links. We do not force users to upload personal data. You stay in control of what gets scanned.

Transparency

Proxuma does not hide behind vague warnings. We show users why links are flagged through visible signals like suspicious domains, redirect chains, phishing indicators, and scam language patterns. Our goal is protection through understanding—not blind trust.
