Privacy First Threat Detection

Proxuma helps you inspect suspicious links, understand warning signs, and choose the right depth of protection without turning your browsing into someone else’s data stream.

What Proxuma Stands For

Privacy means your scan should not become a tracking event.

Transparency means a warning should explain itself, not hide behind a mystery score.

Protection means the tool should help you make a safer decision before you click.

Core Principles

Privacy

Your scans stay yours. Proxuma is built around offline-first analysis wherever possible, reducing unnecessary exposure of the links you check.

Transparency

Threat detection should be explainable. Proxuma shows signals, patterns, and reasoning so users understand the verdict.

Protection

Proxuma is designed to slow down risky clicks, reveal suspicious behavior, and give users a clearer path before acting.
