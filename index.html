See what links are really doing.

Proxuma is a small set of tools designed to help people understand suspicious links — not by blocking them or tracking users, but by explaining what’s happening in plain language.

Everything runs locally. Nothing phones home. No panic. Just clarity.

Proxuma Lite

Quick checks for everyday browsing. Designed for fast, no-nonsense insight when something feels off.

Deeper local analysis using structural and contextual heuristics. Breaks risk into credential theft, financial risk, and exploitation — and explains why.
