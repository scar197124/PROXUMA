About Proxuma

Security Should Be Clear

Proxuma was built for people who want practical protection without unnecessary tracking, confusing warnings, or scattered tools.

Why It Exists

Most people see suspicious links every day but do not always know what makes them dangerous. Proxuma gives users a clearer way to inspect links, understand signals, and decide what to do next.

Offline-First Direction

Where possible, analysis runs locally in the browser so the link being checked does not need to be sent somewhere else.

Explainable Results

A verdict should be useful. Proxuma aims to show the reason behind risk instead of only displaying a score.

One Platform

Lite, Sense, and Shield now live inside one Proxuma system with consistent navigation and a clear progression of depth.

Privacy. Transparency. Protection.
