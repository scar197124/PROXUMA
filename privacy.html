Proxuma Privacy Transparency

Privacy

Proxuma is built around clarity without unnecessary exposure. The homepage is intended as a lightweight routing layer into the live mode environments.

Core approach

  • Privacy-first interaction is a design goal, not an afterthought.
  • The homepage does not need to collect personal information to do its job.
  • Users are directed into purpose-built mode environments only when they choose to continue.
  • Any future changes to data handling should be clearly disclosed before use.

Practical note

Because Proxuma routes into live mode environments, privacy details can also vary slightly by environment. Where that happens, the mode-specific page should make that clear inside its own interface.

Proxuma — privacy by direction and restraint.
